Mjolnir Meatball

Home

Description

Thor's classic Mjolnir Meatball, sure to win over the hearts of all your guests.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Egg
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Shape the meat into 2 inch balls
  3. Bake on a prepared baking sheet for 20 minutes
