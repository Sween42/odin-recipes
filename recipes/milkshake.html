Jormungandr Milkshake

Home

Description

What better way to finish off Ragnarok than with this decadent milkshake.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Milk
  • Food coloring
  • Assorted candies

Steps

  1. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extraqct together in a blender until smooth
  2. Mix in food coloring
  3. Pour into glasses and top with candies
