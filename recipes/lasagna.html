Odin's Lasagna

Home

Description

A wonderful lasagna fit for the norse gods.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. In a baking dish place a layer of noodles.
  2. Add a layer of cheese and meat.
  3. Repeat layers of noodles and filing.
  4. Bake in oven at 400F untill done.
